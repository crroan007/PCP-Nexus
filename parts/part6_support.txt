## 16 Troubleshooting
Common issues and how to resolve them.

### Engine Stalled
Wait 2 minutes. If no recovery, restart the app from the desktop shortcut or via the PCP-StartNexus scheduled task.

### No Handover
Ensure the PC was on at 9:45 PM. Check network share connection. The handoff polls every 5 minutes independently of the engine.

### Missing Emails
Likely a folder configuration mismatch. Contact admin to verify the mailbox and folder paths in Settings.

### "Phase 2: e-filing folders unavailable"
Outlook has lost its connection to the efiling shared mailbox. Close and reopen Outlook, then restart PCP Nexus. All emails remain safe in the mailbox - no data is lost. This typically occurs after a network interruption or Outlook session timeout.

### NO_LEAD Exception (all emails present)
Clear the PCP-Packetized tag from the emails in the AI Exceptions or input folder, then restart the engine. The system will re-scan and reprocess them on the next cycle. In v4.5.2+, this scenario is largely prevented by the late-arriving document fix and stabilization delay.

```
PCP-Packetized
```

### "No Logs" on Dashboard Exceptions
Resolved in v4.5.2. Exception items now show full error details and recovery steps. For older items created before v4.5.2, the resolution is the same: clear the PCP-Packetized tag from the source emails to reprocess.

## 17 Operational Checklists

- Confirm engine status is Running (green)
- Verify Dashboard is updating with new filings
- Review the Exceptions folder for flagged items
- Check Manual Review Queue on Dashboard
- Resolve any keyword-flagged alerts
- Verify previous night's handover completed

- Spot-check CSV accuracy against delivered PDFs
- Verify network share paths are accessible
- Review database record counts vs. CSV rows
- Check log files for recurring warnings
- Confirm Outlook mailbox connection is stable
